Our behavioral health care practice offers services to low-income, migrant, and refugee communities in the Greater Boston area and Central Mass. As a tax-exempt organization in Roxbury, our focus is to provide cost-effective, quality treatment to a wide spectrum of society that may not have acess to quality, comprehensive care. We offer in-home behavioral health care to children, adolescents, young proffessionals, and families to help them cope with inner and enviromental challenges.


3) Commonwealth Mental Health and Wellness Center
895 Blue Hill Ave. Boston, MA
cmhwc.com

At CMHWC we employ a holistic approach that intergrates traditional & non-traditional methods of therapy. Our model is one that empowers clients by facilitating a positive collaboration between them, their community supports &our team to improve behavioral health & social needs. We believe CMHWC can provide you with the highest quality of care by providing therapeutic interventions that reduce or eliminate barriers by providing culturally, age, and gender appropriate treatment.
